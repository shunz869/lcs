  • WHO WE ARE

    We are high quality & independent specialist professional cleaning company based in Perth WA. We are passionate cleaners looking after your sweet home and offices. Our experienced team members are trustworthy and reliable, all of whom have been police-cleared, identity checked, professionally trained.

  • WHAT WE DO

    We provide great customer service to our clients on time and with efficiency. We believe in listening to our clients and delivering them exactly what they want, at a surprisingly affordable rate. We are not the cheapest cleaners in town, but we would argue that we are among the best value for money. We provide all our own equipment and products unless your preference suggests otherwise. Our reputation is developed over the past 11 years and is built on trust and respect.

  • OUR Philosophy PHILOSOPHY

    My mum is a housewife who raised three children. She always kept our home sparkling clean; every day when we step into the house, we feel so refreshed and Rejuvenated. She has this magic that makes us feel so warm. We would like to share this rejuvenation effect with your sweet home. Our business started when our neighbour needed cleaning service, then our magic spread from mouth to mouth. We gradually grew our team of cleaners with our trusted friends. We have now developed a loyal and growing client base of both business and residential.
